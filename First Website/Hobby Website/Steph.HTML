    • Chef Curry

Steph Curry, has influenced the game incredibly since coming into the league. He is the best outright shooter to have ever played in the NBA with a 3-point field goal percentage of 43.3%. His ability to shoot from behind the 3-point line and accumulate a high number of points per game changed the way teams in the league played the game. 3-point shooters became a necessary asset for every team and the inside pull up jumpers took a bit of a hit, some refering to it as a lost art in the game now because of Curr's influence. Nicknamed "Chef Curry" due to always being able to cook up a storm against defenses one half of "The Splash Bros" with Klay Thompson another prolific shooter on The Golden State Warriors.
